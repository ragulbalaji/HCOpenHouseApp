Why Hwa Chong?

Through the years, Hwa Chong Institution has nurtured leaders for the nation in the highest echelons of research, industry and government. Our aim is to maximise every student’s potential, and to prepare them for life in the new global age.

At the heart of the Hwa Chong programme is the emphasis we place on moral and character education. The best of Chinese values and Western ideas that have had a strong impact on society are infused into our school curriculum and culture.

LIVE WITH PASSION
& LEAD WITH COMPASSION

We want to engender passion and compassion in Hwa Chong students; to hone them into leaders who are not only responsible to self, family and country, but who can also help sustain humanity and transform society.

The vibrant Hwa Chong community is a close-knit one, with school leaders, staff, teachers, students and alumni coming together to form one large Hwa Chong Family.

A commitment to excellence is a hallmark of Hwa Chong, whose mission is to nurture leaders for the nation. Besides producing some of the nation’s top scholars at the GCE A-Level Exams, our students have also brought pride to Singapore and the school with 46 World Championship titles from 2006-2015.

With exciting initiatives in education, HCI is constantly at the frontier of educational change and innovation.
